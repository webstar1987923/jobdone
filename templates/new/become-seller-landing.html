FAQ

What can I sell?

Be creative! You can offer any service you wish as long as it's legal and complies with our terms. There are many categories you can browse to get ideas.

How much money can I make?

It's your decision. You can work as much as you want. Many sellers work on Jobdone full time and some keep their job while using Jobdone to make extra money.

How much does it cost?

It's free to join Jobdone. There is no subscription required or fees to list your services. You keep 80% of each transaction.

How much time will I need to invest?

It's very flexible. You need to put in some time and effort in the beginning to learn the marketplace and then you can decide for yourself what amount of work you want to do.

How do I price my service?

With Service Extra, you set your pricing anywhere from $5 - $999

How do I get paid?

Once you complete a buyer's order, the money is transferred to your account. No need to chase clients for payments and wait 60 or 90 days for a check.
